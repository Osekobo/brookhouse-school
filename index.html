International School in Runda, Nairobi

Day School in Runda, Brookhouse

The Brookhouse Runda campus opened its doors to learners in 2017. Our sister school is situated on a picturesque site north of Nairobi and offers first-class opportunities for our diverse student community. Children follow the British education system and the School delivers academic excellence in all subjects. Students are encouraged to adopt an exploratory approach to learning in outdoor and indoor settings.

Runda Campus

The Runda School is set upon extensive landscaped grounds in the northern suburbs of Nairobi overlooking the Evergreen Park Lake. The purpose-built campus features sizeable central fields, with the imposing main building rising from a hilltop overlooking picturesque pastures.

On the far side of the School site, the indoor sports centre and swimming pool lead to a state-of-the-art performance theatre. The School also offers cutting-edge technology throughout modern performing arts studios, STEM labs, and sports grounds.
